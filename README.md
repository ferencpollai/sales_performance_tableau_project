https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/ferenc.p.llai/vizzes

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Tableau dashboard designed to analyze **sales performance and customer behavior**.

The solution consists of two main dashboards:

* **Sales Dashboard** – focused on revenue, profit, and trends
* **Customer Dashboard** – focused on customer behavior and engagement

The goal is to support stakeholders such as **sales managers and executives** in making data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

### Sales Analysis

* Analyze year-over-year sales performance
* Identify trends in sales, profit, and quantity
* Compare product subcategories
* Detect high and low performing periods

### Customer Analysis

* Understand customer purchasing behavior
* Analyze order frequency and engagement
* Identify high-value customers
* Evaluate customer distribution patterns

---

## 📁 Dataset

The project is based on a **public transactional dataset** containing:

* Customer information
* Order data
* Sales and profit metrics
* Product categories and subcategories
* Geographic data (region, state, city)

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

* Tableau Public
* CSV data sources

## 📈 Key KPIs

### Sales KPIs

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Total Quantity

### Customer KPIs

* Total Customers
* Total Orders
* Sales per Customer

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 Sales Dashboard

* KPI overview (current year vs previous year)
* Monthly trends for sales, profit, and quantity
* Product subcategory comparison
* Weekly trends with average benchmarks
* Top 10 customers by profit

### 🔹 Customer Dashboard

* KPI overview (customers, orders, sales per customer)
* Monthly customer trends
* Customer distribution by number of orders
* Top 10 customers by profit

---

## 🎛 Interactivity

The dashboards include:

* Dynamic year selection
* Interactive filters:

  * Category & Subcategory
  * Region, State, City
* Clickable charts for filtering
* Navigation between dashboards

---

## 💡 Key Insights

* A small number of customers generate a significant portion of profit
* Sales and profit show clear seasonal patterns
* Customer order frequency varies widely, indicating different engagement levels
* Certain product subcategories outperform others consistently
